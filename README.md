# Oyun-bazari-statistik-gestericileri-Gaming-market-statistics


Oyun bazarı statistik göstəricilərinə aid olan ümumi hesabatlar: Sum,Average, Count və s. .\\ General reports on gaming market statistics: Sum, Average, Count, etc.
This dataset presents unofficial statistics covering the gaming sector—including manufacturers and genres—from 1980 to 2019. While the specific figures listed here do not reflect actual data, all other parameters (such as genres, regions, and manufacturers) are real. Regions are categorized into North America, Japan, Europe, and "Other." The dataset comprises over 15,000 rows. Following data cleaning, Power BI and specific DAX functions were used to calculate metrics such as total sales (by region and overall), average sales, and more.
